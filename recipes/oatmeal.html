Overnight Oats

Rolled oats mixed with peanut butter, honey, and water to be a perfect creamy snack overnight.

Ingredients

  • Rolled Oats
  • Honey
  • Peanut Butter
  • Water
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Put rolled oats of your choice into container
  2. Scoop out peanut butter about 1/3 of the oats (more for thicker, less for watery)
  3. Pour honey of your choice (don't put too much, use your intuition)
  4. Sprinkle some cinnamon
  5. Water of your choice (more water = watery!)
  6. Mix well and store in fridge overnight
  7. Done! Make sure to keep trying if you want a perfect consistency for yourself
