Basic Egg

Pan-fried egg cooked through in and out. Crispy exterior and a fluffy interior.

Ingredients
  • Oil of your choice (I use olive)
  • Egg(s)
Steps
  1. Heat up oil until hot (before it begins to smoke) on preferrably a frying pan
  2. Crack eggshell and drop in egg
  3. Let it stay on one side for approximately 2 minutes
  4. Turn heat to low or off
  5. Flip egg and let it cook for same time
  6. Done
