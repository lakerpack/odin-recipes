Fried potatoes

Perfectly cooked but crispy potatoes for an easy meal!

Ingredients

  • Oil of your choice (I use olive oil)
  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Rosemary or Cilantro

Steps

  1. Get pot of water to boil
  2. Dump potatoes and let them cook for 8 minutes
  3. Take them out and let them air dry on plate
  4. Heat up oil in pan
  5. Put potatoes in pan let them brown
  6. Sprinkle salt, ground black pepper, and paprika
  7. Brown all sides as much as you can
  8. Finish with rosemary or cilantro
  9. Done
